# OKMAYA REALMS Whitepaper

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OKMAYA REALMS, a browser-based RPG minigame ecosystem built on the Base blockchain. This document outlines the vision, gameplay mechanics, tokenomics, and roadmap for the project.

## 2. Gameplay

### 2.1. Core Features
- Turn-based combat system
- NFT character progression
- Guilds & PvP tournaments
- Daily quests & rewards
- Marketplace for rare items
- Cross-platform compatibility

### 2.2. Game Loop
- **Battle:** Fight monsters in various dungeons.
- **Earn:** Collect $OKY tokens and rare NFT items.
- **Upgrade:** Enhance your character and equipment.
- **Trade:** Buy and sell items on the marketplace.
- **Socialize:** Join guilds and participate in community events.

## 3. Tokenomics ($OKY)

### 3.1. Utility
- In-game purchases
- Guild creation and management
- Tournament entry fees
- NFT crafting and upgrading
- Staking for passive rewards

### 3.2. Distribution
- **Total Supply:** 1 Billion $OKY
- **40%:** Game rewards & player incentives
- **20%:** Development & updates
- **15%:** Marketing & partnerships
- **15%:** Liquidity pool
- **10%:** Team (24-month vesting)
